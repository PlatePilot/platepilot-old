About Plate Pilot

In a world where spontaneous supermarket trips often lead to impulse purchases and a repetitive cycle of frozen pizza or noodles with pesto dinners, this tool trys to transform your clinary experience.

Why plan your meals? It's not just about saving money, it's a move towards minimizing food waste and breaking free from the monotony of repetitive meals. With this tool meal planning becomes a breeze.

The Problem

Many individuals find themselves caught in the cycle of unplanned supermarket visits, leading to impulsive purchase and a lack of planning before the trip.

Challenges:
Excessive Spending: Without a meal plan, there's a tendency to buy items on a whim, ultimately leading to higher grocery bills.
Lack of Meal Variety: The absence of a structured plan often results in a repetitive and limited selection of meals, hindering culinary diversity and enjoyment.
Poor Nutritional Choices: The absence of intentional meal planning may lead to an increased reliance on convenient, processed foods, potentially compromising nutritional quality.

The Solution

Plate Pilot trys to provide an effiecient way to plan meals.

How it works

First of, you have to create a new meal or "plate". After that, you can either save the plate or shedule it to a certain date.

What is a "plate"?

A "plate" is bascially just a meal. A meal you plan to prepare and eat. But when you read "plate" on this page, i mean not just a basic meal. The reason it's called plate is, that you can add drinks, accesories and a description. So its more like a recipe, than a meal.

How to add new plates

You have to click on "Add Plate", after that, you can create a name, add other categories (like drinks or accesories) and then add items in those categories. After that you can either save the plate or shedule it to a certain date.

What are saved plates?

Saved plates are the plates, you've saved. So whenever you want, you can choose one of your saved plates and shedule it to a date. You can always edit those saved plates.

Why are there ToBuy lists?

ToBuy lists are for the times, when you need to remember, what you still have to buy. When you check one of the items in a ToBuy list, it doesn't get deleted. This is, so that you can see what you already have baught. If you want to delete an item, you can just click on the x and puff, its gone.

How to use the Fridge

The Fridge is the place, where you can add all the things (food), you have stored in your house. With that, you do not forget, what you still have (or don't have). You can add a name, the amount, an expiration date and a category you want to add the item to.

Why is there a calendar

The calendar is there, so you can have an overview of when you have planned your plates.
